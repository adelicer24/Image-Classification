Image Classifier

Description

  • This website allows our visitors to put our Image Classification algorithm to the test
  • Our algorithm has been trained to identify a set objects such as dogs, cats, cars, ships, and more
  • Our visitors will have the ability to choose an image to be processed by our algorithm
  • Our algorithm will identify what the image is and report it back to our visitors
  • Our visitors can test as many images as they'd like!
